VYEPTI has flexible infusion options based on whether you refer patients to an infusion site or infuse in-office

VYEPTI Infusion Network

The VYEPTI Infusion Network is a nationwide network of infusion centers that administer VYEPTI. Additional sites of infusion are also available. Some infusion providers may be able to offer home infusion.

Infusion options are subject to your patient's insurance benefit.

  • Aveanna Healthcare
  • Coram CVS/Specialty Infusion Services

In-Office Infusion (Buy and Bill)

For practices that infuse in-office, VYEPTI can be purchased through specialty distributors and billed to the patient's medical benefit.

Referring Out to an Infusion Site

If you prefer to refer patients to an infusion center, the VYEPTI Infusion Network and other sites of infusion are available across the country.
