Tips for telling your story

New to storytelling? Follow these 8 tips to tell your story as effectively as possible.

Be yourself. Being real is the key.

Stick to the highlights. Talk about one or two topics regarding your experience with VYEPTI.

Include specific examples. Anecdotes can help bring your story to life.

Keep it short. About 1 minute or 500 characters max.

Be natural. If you're filming a video, try not to read off a script. Act like you're telling a friend about your experience.

Keep it focused. Don’t reference other migraine medications.

Less is more. Leave out anything you’re uncomfortable sharing with a large audience, especially identifying information like your last name or address.

Light matters. Make sure you are in a well-lit room.
