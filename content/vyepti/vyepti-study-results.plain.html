Proven to reduce monthly migraine days in clinical studies

How well VYEPTI works to prevent migraine attacks in the strict environment of a clinical study.

People in the study

1072 people with chronic migraine and 665 people with episodic migraine.

Dosing

People took either VYEPTI 100 mg, VYEPTI 300 mg, or placebo. No one in the study knew which dose they were taking.

Goal of the studies

How well VYEPTI works to prevent migraine attacks over 3 months (1 dose) in the strict environment of a clinical study.

People in the studies

1072 people with chronic migraine and 665 people with episodic migraine.

Dosing

People received either VYEPTI 100 mg, VYEPTI 300 mg, or placebo. No one in the study knew which dose they were receiving.

People with chronic migraine

who averaged 16 migraine days a month receiving VYEPTI* had an average of

8 fewer

migraine days a month

vs 6 with placebo, over 3 months

*VYEPTI 100 mg or 300 mg

Chronic migraine is characterized by 15 or more headache days per month, with at least 8 of those being migraine days.

People with episodic migraine

who averaged 9 migraine days a month receiving VYEPTI* had an average of

4 fewer

migraine days a month

vs 3 with placebo, over 3 months

*VYEPTI 100 mg or 300 mg

Episodic migraine is characterized by 4 to 14 headache days per month, with at least 4 of those being migraine days.

In the same chronic migraine study, after receiving VYEPTI for 6 months (2 doses)*

Up to 40% of people who averaged 16 migraine days a month achieved

zero

migraine days

for a month† or more

40% on VYEPTI 300 mg, 35% on VYEPTI 100 mg, and 22% on placebo had zero migraine days for a month or more.

*This analysis was not part of the original study plan and was initiated after the study ended, so we cannot make any firm conclusions based on these results.

†28 days in a row.

Results of a 2-year clinical study in patients with chronic migraine

Study goal

Study the safety of VYEPTI 300 mg and other patient-reported results over 2 years.

People in the study

128 people with chronic migraine.

Dosing

Everyone in the study knew they were receiving VYEPTI 300 mg, which may have influenced the results.

Safety results were similar to the original clinical trials

more than 70%

felt overall migraine improvement over 2 years*

nearly 60%

felt engaged in life at 2 years†

*Based on a question asking people how they feel about change in their overall migraine status, ranging from very much worse/much worse to much improved/very much improved. Improvement includes much improved or very much improved.

†Based on a questionnaire developed to measure the disability headaches cause in people's lives. Results were measured by the number of disability days reported in the past 3 months. Engaged in life was defined to include little or no disability (0-5 disability days reported).

Adults pictured in images above are real VYEPTI patients who were compensated for their time. These patients did not participate in the study.
