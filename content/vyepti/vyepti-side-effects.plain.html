VYEPTI Side Effects

A treatment with few
side effects? Yep!

VYEPTI safety in clinical studies

Less than 2% of more than 1100 people stopped receiving VYEPTI due to side effects in clinical studies. The most common side effects were:

  • Stuffy nose and scratchy throat (6–8%)
  • Allergic reactions (1–2%)

Long-term safety

Side effects were similar when VYEPTI was tested over 2 years in an additional study of 128 people with chronic migraine. Discontinuation due to side effects was 6%.

These are not all the possible side effects of VYEPTI. Please read the Patient Information for more details. Tell your healthcare provider if you have any side effect that bothers you or that does not go away.

Tell your healthcare provider if you become pregnant or plan to become pregnant.
