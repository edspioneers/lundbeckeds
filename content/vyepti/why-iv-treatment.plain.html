Why IV Treatment

Migraine prevention
delivered differently

Discover intravenous (IV) infusion

Just four 30-minute IV infusions a year (every 3 months) given by a healthcare provider.

100% of the medication is delivered into your bloodstream, so it can get right to work.

86% of people receiving VYEPTI agreed that their infusion experience was positive

Based on survey responses from 713 people in a VYEPTI support program through December 2023.
Individual experiences may vary. Survey responses collected between 4/1/21 and 12/15/23.

0:08 VYEPTI is different.​

0:13 VYEPTI is the first and only FDA-approved intravenous (IV) infusion for the preventive treatment of migraine.​

0:20 It's delivered to your body in a different way...

0:22 …as a 30-minute IV infusion given just 4 times a year.

0:28 VYEPTI is designed to work fast. During each 30-minute infusion, 100% of the medication goes directly to your bloodstream and begins circulating.

0:35 And just one infusion can provide 23 fewer migraine days over 3 months.

0:40 Now let’s take a closer look at how VYEPTI works and what you can expect during and after treatment.

0:47 Inside the body, there’s a protein called calcitonin gene-related peptide, or CGRP. CGRP attaches to and activates specific receptors, which may play a role in migraine.

1:02 VYEPTI blocks CGRP and prevents it from attaching to and activating the receptors.

1:07 So what’s the infusion experience like? The infusion location can be flexible. Depending on your insurance, you can receive your VYEPTI treatment at an infusion center, at your doctor’s office, or even at home.

1:22 Once you’ve gotten comfortable in your infusion chair, you’re in the hands of healthcare professionals—just sit back and relax for the next 30 minutes.

1:32 When your infusion is complete, there's no downtime. You can go about your day and get back to your normal activities.

1:40 Just be sure to schedule your next infusion before you wrap up, and space treatments no more than 3 months apart.

1:45 Now what kind of results might you experience after treatment?

1:54 With VYEPTI, more people had migraine-free days during the first week after treatment—some as soon as Day 1.

2:00 And the results are lasting. VYEPTI keeps working through month 3 after each infusion. That’s why you only get an IV infusion treatment 4 times a year. Common side effects on VYEPTI are allergic reactions, stuffy nose, and scratchy throat.

2:15 And if you have any questions between treatments, no problem—the VYEPTI nurse support program lets you talk one-on-one with a registered nurse.

2:25 Ask your migraine specialist if VYEPTI could make a difference for you.

2:28 And that's not all. Stick around for the full important safety information.
