Let’s talk costs—and how you may be able to save

The list price* of VYEPTI is $2,004.09 for a 100 mg/mL vial. But the amount you pay can vary, depending on your insurance plan and any financial assistance you may receive. That’s why different people may pay different amounts for the same drug.

Regardless of price, VYEPTI patient support is focused on helping lower out-of-pocket costs for eligible patients.

*The recommended dose is 100 mg every 3 months. There may be additional costs for the infusion and other fees, which will vary by healthcare provider, site of care, and insurance. List price is also referred to as wholesale acquisition cost, or WAC.

More support is available to help you get started on VYEPTI

Once you've been prescribed VYEPTI, enroll in VYEPTI CONNECT® for support along your treatment journey.* Just complete and sign the enrollment form, then email it to enrollment@lundbeck.com.

VYEPTI CONNECT can:

  • Work with your healthcare provider and insurance to help you understand your coverage for VYEPTI
  • Confirm insurance coverage for an infusion location that works for you: your doctor's office, an infusion center, a hospital outpatient clinic, or even in your home—including providers that are part of the VYEPTI Infusion Network. Note that the cost of infusion may vary based on provider.

*VYEPTI CONNECT is only available for patients who have been prescribed VYEPTI and who are enrolled in VYEPTI CONNECT; support may vary based on patient eligibility. Please see the full Terms and Conditions.

Commercial insurance: coverage I get through work or pay for myself

There are many types of commercial health plans that provide varying types of coverage, either through your employer or that you purchased yourself.

The most common options include:

  • Health Maintenance Organization (HMO)
  • Preferred Provider Organization (PPO)
  • Federal Employee Health Plan (FEP)
  • Point-of-Service (POS) plan
  • Fee-for-Service (FFS) plan
  • High-Deductible Health Plan (HDHP)

If you have commercial insurance, VYEPTI offers a copay assistance program that can help eligible patients pay as little as $0 per infusion every 3 months.* Offer includes up to $200 in administration out-of-pocket fees per VYEPTI treatment.

Medicare: coverage for those who are over 65 or have a qualifying disability

Medicare Part B helps cover medically necessary doctor visits, diagnostic testing, and other preventive care. It also covers physician-administered drugs that could be given in a doctor's office, hospital outpatient setting, or infusion center.

With Medicare Part B, you may be responsible for a 20% coinsurance payment for VYEPTI after you fulfill your deductible. If you have a Medicare supplemental insurance plan (also called Medigap), or a Medicare Advantage Plan (Medicare Part C), your out-of-pocket costs may be lower. These plans help cover some of your out-of-pocket expenses not paid by Medicare.

Call 1-800-MEDICARE (1-800-633-4227) or check with your insurance provider if you have any questions.
