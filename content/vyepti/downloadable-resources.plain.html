Community Resources

Explore some local and national migraine advocacy and community organizations.

American Migraine Foundation

Mobilizes the community for patient support and advocacy and supports impactful research that translates into advances for people with migraine and other disabling diseases that cause severe head pain.

Association of Migraine Disorders

Strives to expand the understanding of migraine and its true scope by supporting research, education, and awareness.

CHAMP

Improves the lives of people with migraine, cluster, and other headache diseases by aligning coalition members and empowering patient voices.

Chronic Migraine Awareness, Inc.

The voice of the chronic migraine community, offering support groups and teaching you how to advocate for yourself and others.

Miles for Migraine

Hosts Walk/Run/Relax events, education events, support groups, and volunteer groups. Supports headache fellowship programs to increase research and foster advocacy for adults and children.

National Headache Foundation

The oldest and largest foundation for people living with migraine disease and headache disorders. NHF’s focus is education, advocacy, research, and furthering awareness of headache and migraine as legitimate neurobiological diseases.

World Health Education Foundation

Serves those who live with chronic illness and their caregivers. Specializes in migraine initiatives that help provide patients with affordable access to care, better education, and advocacy.
