How VYEPTI Works

Designed to help prevent migraine attacks

What is VYEPTI?

VYEPTI is the 1st and only FDA-approved preventive treatment for migraine given as an intravenous (IV) infusion.

Gets right to work

As an IV infusion, 100% of the medication is delivered into your bloodstream, so it can get right to work.

Purposefully designed

VYEPTI was purposefully designed as an IV infusion to start working fast and last through month 3 after each infusion.

How VYEPTI works

VYEPTI is an aCGRP, a type of medication designed specifically for the prevention of migraine.

aCGRPs are recommended by the American Headache Society (AHS) to be one of the first types of preventive migraine treatment prescribed.

  • Calcitonin gene-related peptide, or CGRP, is a protein in the body thought to be a key cause of migraine, triggering attacks when it binds with its receptor
  • VYEPTI binds to CGRP, holding on tightly and stopping it from binding to its receptor
The relationship between your body’s response to the medication and how VYEPTI prevents migraine is not known.
