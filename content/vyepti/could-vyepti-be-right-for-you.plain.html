The battle with migraine disability

Let’s face it—no matter how often you get migraine attacks, living with migraine can be a constant struggle.
Migraine can make you feel like you’re:
  • Pushing through pain again and again
  • Unable to make or stick to plans
  • Struggling to keep up with daily responsibilities
  • Barely making it through the day, let alone enjoying life

People with migraine deserve better

67% of people who have at least 8 monthly migraine days say having their migraine under control feels impossible.*
Because of insurance coverage rules, people with migraine often need to try certain types of treatments before others. According to the American Headache Society (AHS), this can keep people from finding the right treatment for them sooner.
*In a 2021 National Headache Foundation survey of 1144 people with chronic migraine.

Possible signs your current treatment plan may not be right for you:

  • Your migraine or headache days have worsened over the last 3 months
  • Your treatment doesn't seem to work as well as it used to
  • You take more rescue meds

Talk to your doctor about preventive treatment options

If you're still feeling the impact of migraine, ask your doctor if preventive treatment could be right for you. aCGRPs like VYEPTI are designed specifically for the prevention of migraine. And not all aCGRPs are the same, even if you've tried one before.
Ask a doctor if VYEPTI could be right for you. Look up your insurance coverage to find out if VYEPTI may be covered by your plan.
