From difficulty working to more migraine-free days

"I've been a migraine sufferer for 5+ years and tried numerous medications. I suffered with migraine attacks almost every other day; some weeks I would go days at a time, making it difficult to work. My doctor recommended VYEPTI and I was open to anything at that point. I have now had my first infusion.
